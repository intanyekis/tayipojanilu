John Legend And Jennifer Hudson Teamed Up To Sing Beauty And The Beast. Fans Are Obsessed.

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 13, 2024

ABC’s Disney Family Singalong has been dazzling viewers lately.

Between Halsey’s rendition of Part Of Your World and Idina Menzel and Ben Platt’s version of A Whole New World, we’ve witnessed some truly remarkable performances on the show.

However, according to InStyle, nothing quite compares to the reaction fans have had to Jennifer Hudson and John Legend’s Sunday night performance of Beauty and the Beast.

Fans Are “Still Shaking”

Following the duo’s performance, fans jumped on Twitter to express their love for the moment.

“I swear... Jennifer Hudson and John Legend were meant to sing Disney songs,” tweeted one fan.

While another person claimed that they were “still shaking,” after the rendition.

Indeed, it really was a touching tribute to the classic Disney tune. One fan even asked if they can vote for them.

Take a listen yourself and you’ll feel the goosebumps appearing.

It Didn’t End There

As confirmed by E, The show also featured an outstanding performance of Dumbo’s Baby Mine from Katy Perry who even dressed up for the part, as well as The Little Mermaid’s Poor Unfortunate Souls by Rebel Wilson.

Derek Hough, Julianne Hough and Hayley Erbert also danced to Mary Poppins’ Step in Time and other classic Disney songs.

Legend Is Back In Full Form

In addition to sweeping crowds away with his latest Disney Singalong duet with Hudson, John Legend has also recently released new music.

As reported by TheThings, Legend announced his collaboration with Buju Banton last week on Twitter.

The song is called Memories.
