The Highest-Grossing Movies And TV Shows Of Sharon Stone, Ranked

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Quick Links

  • Sphere - $50.1 Million
  • Catwoman - $82 Million
  • Casino - $110.4 Million
  • Last Action Hero - $137.2 Million
  • Antz - $152.4 Million
  • The Specialist - $170.3 Million
  • Total Recall - $261.3 Million
  • Basic Instinct - $352.6 Million

Born in Meadville, Pennsylvania, Sharon Stone began her career as a model before transitioning to acting. With a career spanning over three decades, she has appeared in over 100 films and television shows, amassing an impressive total box office gross of over $1.5 billion. Stone's notable works include her iconic roles in Basic Instinct, Casino, and Total Recall, which have earned her critical acclaim and numerous accolades, including a Golden Globe Award and an Academy Award nomination.

From her unforgettable performances to captivating storylines, Sharon Stone’s filmography showcases her incredible range and undeniable star power.

Sphere - $50.1 Million

In this 1998 science fiction thriller, Sharon Stone plays Dr. Elizabeth ‘Beth’ Halperin, a biochemist and part of a team sent to investigate a mysterious spacecraft discovered on the ocean floor. Directed by Barry Levinson, Sphere also stars Dustin Hoffman, Samuel L. Jackson, and Liev Schreiber. With a budget of $80 million, the film received mixed reviews and turned out to be a box office dud managing to gross only $50.1 Million worldwide.

Catwoman - $82 Million

Sharon Stone took on the role of the villainous Laurel Hedare in the 2004 superhero film Catwoman. Starring Halle Berry, the movie follows the story of a shy woman who transforms into a confident and powerful Catwoman after a near-death experience. Directed by Pitof, Catwoman had a budget of $100 million and bombed at the box office, grossing only $82 million worldwide after receiving highly negative reviews from critics.

Casino - $110.4 Million

In Martin Scorsese's 1995 crime drama Casino, Sharon Stone delivered a stunning performance as Ginger McKenna, the wife of a casino owner played by Robert De Niro. The film, which also stars Joe Pesci, is based on the true story of the rise and fall of a casino empire in Las Vegas. With a budget of $52 million, Casino was a box office success, earning $110.4 million worldwide and garnering widespread critical acclaim. For her role as Ginger, Stone went on to garner her first and only Academy Award nomination for Best Actress. She also drew an impressive paycheck of $2 Million for the movie as per Celebrity Net Worth.

Last Action Hero - $137.2 Million

In this 1993 action-comedy, Sharon Stone makes a cameo appearance as herself alongside Arnold Schwarzenegger, who plays a fictional action hero named Jack Slater. Directed by John McTiernan, Last Action Hero tells the story of a young boy who gets transported into the world of his favorite action movie. With a budget of $85 million, the film grossed $137.2 million worldwide with a domestic collection of $50 Million, according to The Numbers.

Antz - $152.4 Million

Sharon Stone lent her voice to the character of Princess Bala in the 1998 animated film Antz. Directed by Eric Darnell and Tim Johnson, Antz had a budget of $105 million and grossed $152.4 million worldwide. The movie, which also features the voices of Woody Allen, Gene Hackman, and Sylvester Stallone, follows the story of a neurotic ant who tries to break free from his colony's conformity.

The Specialist - $170.3 Million

In the 1994 action thriller The Specialist, Sharon Stone stars as May Munro, a woman seeking revenge for her parent's murder. She enlists the help of a former CIA explosives expert, played by Sylvester Stallone. Directed by Luis Llosa, the film also stars James Woods and Rod Steiger. With a budget of $45 million, The Specialist turned out to be a box office hit, earning $170.3 million worldwide, as per IMDB. Stone drew an impressive $5 Million paycheck for the movie.

Total Recall - $261.3 Million

In this 1990 science fiction classic, Sharon Stone plays Lori Quaid, the wife of a construction worker who discovers his life is a fabricated memory. Directed by Paul Verhoeven, Total Recall also stars Arnold Schwarzenegger and Rachel Ticotin. With a budget of $65 million, the film was a massive critical and commercial success, grossing $261.3 million worldwide with an astounding domestic gross of $119.4 Million, and went on to become a cult classic according to Box Office Mojo.

Basic Instinct - $352.6 Million

Sharon Stone's most iconic role came in the form of Catherine Tramell, a seductive crime novelist in the 1992 erotic thriller Basic Instinct. The film, directed by Paul Verhoeven, also stars Michael Douglas as a detective investigating a brutal murder. With a budget of $49 million, Basic Instinct was a box office sensation, earning $352.6 million worldwide and catapulting Stone to superstardom. Stone received her first-ever Golden Globe nomination for her impressive role as Catherine. She drew a modest paycheck of $500,000 for Basic Instinct which catapulted to a whopping $13.6 Million for the 2006 sequel, Basic Instinct 2.

From thrilling adventures to captivating dramas, Sharon Stone's highest-grossing movies showcase her ability to generate incredible box office pull. With diverse roles and unforgettable performances, Stone has solidified her place as one of Hollywood's most successful actresses. Basic Instinct, with an astounding worldwide gross of $352.6 Million, is the highest-grossing Sharon Stone movie to date.
