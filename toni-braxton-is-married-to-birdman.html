Fact-Check: No, Toni Braxton Is Not Married To Birdman

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, June 21, 2024

The rumors of the alleged marriage of R&B icon Toni Braxton and rapper Birdman are surfacing across the Internet. However, amidst the enthusiasm of ongoing rumors, the truth appears shrouded.

This fact check delves into the depths of this alleged marriage to discern the truth behind the rumors. Are Toni Braxton and Birdman married?

The Claim

Toni Braxton and Birdman are married in a private ceremony in Mexico.

The Ruling

FALSE

The claim that Toni Braxton and Birdman are secretly married is False, as indicated by multiple reputable and reliable media outlets.

Fact

Toni Braxton and Bryan Christopher Williams aka Birdman have reportedly been dating since 2016. However, their relationship went on and off since the R&B icon and rapper called off their engagement in 2019. Both Braxton and Birdman have been in the limelight once again after the rumors of their marriage surfaced across the internet. 

Evidence

Official Confirmation: The prominent R&B icon, Toni Braxton took her Instagram to address the rumors of her alleged wedding. The star confirmed that she is not married yet. 

Media Outlets: Multiple reputable and reliable media outlets such as People, Entertainment Tonight, and Page Six, have confirmed that Toni is not married to Birdman and both are single now. 

Credibility: We did not find any credible evidence to support the claim of the alleged marriage of R&B star Toni Braxton and rapper Birdman.

Explanation

Rumors about Toni Braxton and Bryan Christopher Williams’ marriage are surfacing across the Internet these days.

Though the couple have been in an on-and-off relationship since they called off their engagement in 2019, the R&B star has confirmed that she is not married to Birdman. 

Toni Braxton Slams Wedding Rumors

Toni Braxton took her Instagram to confirm that she is not married to Birdman. The star claimed that she has never been married to him and both of them are single now. 

Braxton took a screenshot of rumors spreading across the Internet claiming about her secret wedding with Birdman in Mexico, and posted it on her Instagram handle calling it “Fake News.”

The Couple did not clarify their breakup reason

As per the reports, both Braxton and Birdman were all set for their engagement in 2019. However, the couple called off their engagement in 2019 and have been in an on-and-off relationship since then. The couple did not disclose the actual reason for their split.

Birdman and Braxton Sharing a Passionate Kiss in November 2023

As per the reports, Toni Braxton posted a picture of Birdman and her sharing a passionate kiss in November 2023. However, the singer-songwriter later deleted the post from her official profile.

The incident confused people about her actual relationship status and compelled the audience to speculate about their potential romance. 

Conclusion

Despite persistent rumors, Toni Braxton and Birdman’s alleged marriage stands out to be fake. Official statements and reputable media sources confirm their single status, dispelling the speculation encompassing their relationship.

Braxton’s own Instagram denies the claims, labeling them as “FAKE News.” While their on-and-off history sparks interest, the reality stands: The rapper and R&B icon are not married to each other.
