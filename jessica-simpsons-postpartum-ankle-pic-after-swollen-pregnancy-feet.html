Jessica Simpsons Postpartum Ankle Pic After Swollen Pregnancy Feet

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

There they are! Jessica Simpson revealed her postpartum ankles after sharing pics of her swollen pregnancy feet in January.

“I spy… my ankles!!!!” the fashion designer, 38, captioned the Tuesday, June 18, Instagram upload. Taken in bed, the photo featured one of her bare legs.

Many celebs showed their support for the actress. Married at First Sight’s Jamie Otis wrote, “Killer [legs] mama! #inspiration,” while Real Housewives of Beverly Hills‘ Kylie Richards added, “They’re back!”

When she was still carrying her daughter Birdie in January, Simpson asked her Instagram followers for help with her swollen feet. “Any remedies?” she captioned an upload of her left foot at the time. She even shared a throwback shot of her legs in denim shorts and wedge sandals as part of the “#tenyearchallenge.”

Later that month, after cupping her feet, the I Do author shared a progress pic, captioned, “SKANKLES aka Skinny Ankles.”

Simpson and her husband, Eric Johnson, who already share Maxwell, 7, and Ace, 5, welcomed Birdie in March. “We are so happy and proud to announce the birth of our perfect daughter, Birdie Mae Johnson,” the Dukes of Hazzard star announced at the time. “3.19.19. 10 Pounds 13 Ounces.”

She honored the former professional football player, 39, on Sunday, June 16, with a sweet Father’s Day tribute.
