Danielle von Zerneck Married For Years And Living Happily With Her Family!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

If there is anything that made Danielle von Zerneck a successful personality today then it has to be her dedication and hardwork which she showed since the beginning of her career. Worked on both big and small screens, her works are exemplary to many upcomers.

Now a wife and a mother, she has her own sets of responsibilities to fulfill but still, has not backed down her contribution to the cinematic industry just yet. Well, let’s look at more about her personal and professional life!

General Hospital Actress Married With Partner Since A Long Time!

The Tale of Two Sisters star is living a blissful married with her husband James Fearnley. Her partner is also a notable person and is an English musician who played accordion for the Irish band The Pogues.

The couple walked down the aisle in the gathering of their families and friends in 1989. Ever since that day, the duo has been living in holy matrimony and relishing a happy marital life.

From their relaitonship, the pair is blessed with two kids. Both of their children are daughters; Martha Fearnley and Irene Fearnley.

Danielle shares an amazing bond with her family and spends time with them. Likewise, she shares pictures and videos of her activities on her social media.

Rumors of death 

Not long ago, rumors were floating that the actress Danielle Von Zerneck was dead. But as you already know, the My Science Project actress is very much alive and kicking.
