Giants' Sterling Shepard kisses influencer girlfriend in video

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 8, 2024

Giants wide receiver Sterling Shepard and his girlfriend, Carolyn Moore, are going strong heading into the 2023 NFL season.

Moore, an influencer and beauty entrepreneur, revealed in a TikTok video posted Monday that the couple’s love language is physical touch.

“My boyfriend’s beige flag is he can’t walk past me without giving me a kiss or some kind of love tap,” Moore wrote over the video that showed Shepard, 30, giving her a kiss while she was getting her hair done.

In a separate video on her TikTok page, Shepard is seen greeting Moore and her young son, Lion, on the sidelines at MetLife Stadium before the Giants defeated the Panthers in their preseason game last Friday.

“My boys,” Moore wrote in her caption.

The influencer also shared photos from the exhibition game, where she sported Giants apparel.

Shepard went public with the relationship in March when the couple vacationed together in Turks & Caicos.

It came four months after the former second-round pick attended Odell Beckham Jr.’s 30th birthday party with Shaniece Hairston in November.

At the time, Shepard and Hairston — an influencer and yoga teacher — shared photos with each other on their respective Instagram pages, which have since been deleted.

Moore has also shared Instagram photos of Shepard’s two daughters, Cali Clay, 5, and three-year-old Cassie Snow.

Shepard shares his daughters with his ex, model Chanel Iman, who he married in March 2018.

The NFL star filed for divorce in June 2021 and requested joint legal custody of their daughters.

Iman went public with her relationship with Patriots defensive tackle Davon Godchaux in April 2022.

The former Victoria’s Secret model, 32, announced in May that Godchaux, 28, proposed while in Capri, Italy.

It came just a few weeks after they announced they are expecting their first child together.

Iman and Godchaux have since revealed they are expecting a baby girl.

Godchaux is a dad to seven-year-old son Davon Godchaux II from a previous relationship.
