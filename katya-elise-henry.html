Katya Elise Henry Biography, Age, Wiki, Height, Weight, Boyfriend, Family &amp; More

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, June 2, 2024

If we said that Beauty is being the best possible version of yourself and this line is well proved by “Katya Elise Henry” who is an American Instagram star, Social media personality, and fitness model. She is well known for posting her training photos and videos on social media platforms.

As a social media personality, she has more than 7.7 million followers on Instagram, around 394.8k followers on Twitter, and more than 270k followers on Facebook. In addition to this, she started an international health and fitness brand called ‘Workouts by Katya’.

This stunning beauty seems tall with a height of 168 centimeters and 5’6” in feet & inches. Born on, June 14, 1994, this beautiful Diva has turned 29 years old. She possesses a healthy and fit figure with a weight of 53 kilograms and 117 lbs in pounds.

Her body is complemented by her beautiful dark brown eyes and her silky long black hair. She is very conscious about her physique and her figure measurements are 32 inches breast, 24 inches waist, and 38 inches hips. Her bra size is 32B.

She earns from her endorsement deals with several brands and also has quite good earnings from her professional career as a fitness model and Instagram star. According to the sources, the estimated net worth of Katya is $3 Million.

Katya was born and raised in Minneapolis, Minnesota, USA, and belongs to Russian, African American, Native American, and Swedish ancestry. She is the lovely daughter of her father Derick Anthony Henry who was a professional bodyguard for over two decades and her mother Tawnya Lynn Nelson who was a personal trainer.

She also has two lovely sisters named Tanaya Henry and Gabrielle Henry. As per her education, she completed her schooling at Prior Lake High School and then Horizon High School. Later, she attended Desert Mountain High School in Scottsdale, Arizona to complete her graduation.
