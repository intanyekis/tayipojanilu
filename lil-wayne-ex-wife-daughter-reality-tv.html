Lil Wayne's Ex-Wife &amp; Daughter Get Their Own Reality TV Series

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, June 27, 2024

Lil Wayne‘s former wife Toya Johnson-Rushing, along with his eldest daughter Reginae Carter, are currently in production for their own unscripted reality show.

Variety reports AMC Networks’ WE TV has greenlit a new series called Toya & Reginae, starring the ex-wife and daughter of the New Orleans Hip Hop legend. The new unscripted show is a continuation of WE TV’s popular Growing Up Hip Hop franchise, which Johnson-Rushing and Carter have both been involved in.

AD LOADING...

According to the network’s recent press release, the new show will pull back the curtain into the lives of the high profile mother-and-daughter duo, proving that “as happy as they seem on social media, their lives are unpredictable and full of chaos.”

Toya & Reginae will air this fall exclusively on WE TV and sister steamer ALLBLK. “I’m so excited to let viewers back into our lives to see what my family has been up to since you’ve seen us last,” said Johnson-Rushing in a recent statement.

AD LOADING...

On Wednesday (April 19), Toya shared a post from WE TV about the upcoming series on her Instagram Story. Earlier that day, the network had uploaded a photo of the mother-and-daughter duo, with the caption: “We are happy to announce that @toyajohnson and @itsreginaecarter will return to @wetv and @watchallblk soon with their new series #ToyaAndReginae.”

There is also an order for a scripted drama titled Bev is Boss, based on the life of Deb Antney, the mother of Waka Flocka Flame and legendary music management mogul. Antney has been credited with breaking the careers of high-profile artists such as Gucci Mane, French Montana, and Nicki Minaj as the founder and CEO of Mizay Entertainment.

In the scripted series, Antney’s journey from a girl growing up in Queens to becoming an accomplished manager is explored.
