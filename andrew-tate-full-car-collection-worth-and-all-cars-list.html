What shade of Bugatti do you own? Know the entire car collection and its worth of Andrew Tate

On multiple instances, you might have heard these words posted on social media, and Andrew Tate was the voice behind them. In addition to being a well-known figure, Andrew Tate is also a source of controversy among young people and online.

Recently, Top G tweeted that he has 33 cars during the scandal and added, “This is just the start.” Therefore, TSG is here to provide you access to Andrew Tate’s top car collection.

Bugatti Chiron 

Andrew Tate is the owner of the orange-coloured Chiron, one of just 60 in the world. One of his best and most potent vehicles is a Bugatti Chiron, which features an 8.0 litre quad-turbocharged W16 engine, a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, a 400 km/h top speed, and a carbon fibre body. The Bugatti Chiron is currently priced at roughly $3 million, but he paid over $5.2 million for the car’s opulent upgrades and extras.

McLaren 720S

The McLaren 720S in purple, which costs about $400,000, is his second-most costly vehicle. With its convertible body design, 4.0 L V8 Engine, 530 Kw Horsepower, and 7-speed automated transmission. In addition, Andrew Tate frequently drives this vehicle when he travels.

McLaren 765LT

The automobile costs around $400,000 in black and blue colour combination. He upgraded from his previous McLaren 720S by purchasing this second McLaren. Andrew Tate is the owner of one of the rarest McLaren cars in the entire world.

Rolls Royce Wraith

A Rolls Royce always leaves a car collection empty, and Andrew Tate is the proud owner of a Rolls Royce Wraith. He has one of the greatest cars in the world, which costs roughly $380,000 and has four seats, 465 kW horsepower, a 6.6 litre V12 engine, and a Royal Blue colour.

Ferrari 812 Superfast 

Every auto collector loves Ferrari, and he has two of them. The 812 Superfast is his most expensive and potent Ferrari vehicle out of both models. Undoubtedly one of the fastest Ferraris, it comes in Royal blue and has a 6.5-litre V engine, rear-wheel drive, and all of those features.

Ashton Martin DBS Superleggera and the Porsche GT Street

The price of the two vehicles is almost $316,000, but Ashton Martins prevails. Andrew Tate’s automotive collection has a British flair and includes one of the most cherished super luxury vehicles with a 5.2 Litre automatic engine in a blue-greyish colour.

Bentley Continental GT and Mercedes B63 Brabus 

He has a Bentley Continental GT and a Mercedes B63 Brabus, both of which cost about $250,000. Both cars have unique characteristics that set them apart from one another in their respective categories. While travelling to numerous shoots and frequently appearing in his videos, Andrew Tate is frequently spotted in this car.

Lamborghini Huracan Evo Spyder

Lamborghini Huracan Evo Spyder in Pink In the list of cars, this vehicle is the genuine Hurricane. This is one of Tate’s most cherished vehicles among his admirers, who frequently share images of him driving it. In addition, it is unusual to see a Lamborghini in a colour other than the usual red, green, and yellow.

 Ferrari 458 Italia

One of the Ferraris he owns is a 458 Italia, which sells for about $248,000. He drives a red Ferrari that has a 4497 V8 engine and 9,000 rpm of power; it is the ultimate fantasy vehicle for Ferrari enthusiasts. For Andrew Tate, it is also enjoyable.

Ashton Martin DB11

On the other hand, this is his second Ashton Martin, the first costing roughly $240,000. Andrew Tate adores this Ashton Martin with its 5.2 litre V12 engine, 270-litre luggage volume, and 447 kW of horsepower.

In addition to the vehicles stated above, he also owns several luxury and sports vehicles that are not known to the general public. Additionally, he is the owner of a Mercedes-Maybach V-Class, a BMW M5 Competition, and other as-yet-unidentified vehicles.
