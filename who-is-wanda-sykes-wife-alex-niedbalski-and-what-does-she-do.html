Who Is Wanda Sykes' Wife Alex Niedbalski, And What Does She Do?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Wanda Sykes is certainly a name that you have heard of before! The star first came to prominence back in 1998 when she made her film debut in Tomorrow Night. Sykes later found success as an HBO correspondent, and in The Chris Rock Show.

Luckily for Wanda, her career took off immensely come the 2000s, where she was not only performing stand-up across the country but landing movie role after role, including her success in the Jane Fonda and Jennifer Lopez film, Monster In Law. 

With her career continuously booming, Wanda also found success in her personal life when she met her wife, Alex Niedbalski in 2006. The comedian now posts moments of their lives together on her hilarious Instagram account, leaving many fans to wonder who exactly Alex is, and what she does!

Who Is Alex Niedbalski?

Wanda Sykes has been in the entertainment business for quite some time! The star has had immense success as a comic, and actress, with a career spanning well over 20 years. While the star may keep her private life on the down-low, especially when it comes to her twins, Wanda never holds back from telling a hilarious family story!

During Wanda's visits over to The Ellen Show, Sykes always touches on her family drama, mainly with her wife, Alex Niedbalski! While fans know that Alex is French, considering Wanda does a stellar impression, fans don't know much else about her. So, what does Alex do for a living?

Well, prior to meeting Wanda, Niedbalski worked as a kitchen designer, specializing particularly in kitchen countertop sales. While this may not sound like the most riveting job in the world, it's exactly what led Alex to meet Wanda.

In 2006, the duo attended a party on New York's, Fire Island. According to Wanda, she spotted Alex with a computer bag speaking to a little child from a distance, and she instantly felt an attraction.

As the weekend went on, Wanda made it a point to discuss her upcoming kitchen renovations, leaving her friends to set her up with the perfect countertop seller, who just so happened to be the woman Wanda spotted the day before, Alex!

The two dated for 2 years before officially tying the knot in a private ceremony in 2008! This happened only 1 month before Wanda would publically come out as a lesbian while attending a same-sex marriage rally in Las Vegas regarding Proposition 8.

This officially marked the first time the world would be introduced to Wanda and Alex as a couple! In 2009, the duo welcomed their fraternal twins, Olivia and Lucas, who are both bilingual thanks to Wanda's English and Alex's French!

As for Alex and what she does for a living today, well, she remained a supportive partner to Sykes throughout her career and now spends her time as a homemaker, raising the couples' two kids between Los Angeles, Pennsylvania, and France!
