Taylor Swift And Joe Jonas Broke Up 15 Years Ago, But Where Do They Stand Today?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Taylor Swift and Joe Jonas were once the ultimate couple, with fans of the two singers absolutely adoring them and wanting them to stay together forever. However, much to the disappointment of those fans, the two were clearly never meant to be and ended up breaking up.

Given that this all went down over a decade ago and the pair are still very much in the entertainment industry, this might lead many to question where the pair stand with one another today. Especially given the dramatics of the whole situation and what happened afterward, is there any chance of a Joe Jonas and Taylor Swift reunion any time soon?

How Old Was Taylor When She Dated Joe Jonas?

As far as we're aware, Joe was Taylor's first boyfriend (at least in the public eye) and the pair was about 18/19 years old when they were dating. This is still quite young and given this was during a time when the pair were still dealing with the heights of fame, it's understandable why fans were shipping them.

Plus, their fans were still quite young themselves and more than likely were at an age where they were under the impression that relationships last forever, and Taylor is more than proof that this just isn't the case.

How Long Did Taylor Swift Date Joe Jonas?

Whilst at the time their relationship felt like it lasted forever, it actually only lasted for a few months. It's believed that the relationship lasted between July and October 2008, so only actually spanned roughly three months, so really not that long in the grand scheme of things.

Fans of the pair will know how things ended as it was a pretty famous and shocking breakup, as Taylor went on The Ellen DeGeneres Show and said how Joe had broken up with her over the phone in 25 seconds.

Are Taylor And Joe Jonas Friends?

Given how long ago the relationship was and how young they both were, a lot can happen in that time. Taylor went through yet another breakup with former boyfriend Joe Alwyn and also allegedly split from her rebound Matty Healy.

In that time as well, Joe has married Sophie Turner and they are raising their two daughters together. Given that it's more than clear that both Taylor and Joe have moved on from one another, is there any chance that they can mend the rift between them?

The good news is that, whilst it's highly unlikely that the pair will ever date each other again, they are actually on good terms now. Joe revealed on the 'Armchair Expert' podcast back in May the state of his and Taylor's relationship today and stated that they are "cool" with one another.

He also said that any drama that the pair went through is all a thing of the past and that they have finally been able to put it all behind them. Joe also mentioned that he hopes this means he's no longer on the wrong side of Taylor's fans, as "no one f--ks with swifties."

This was first seen when the pair appeared on The Graham Norton Show in 2019 together and appeared to get on quite well. Sophie even shared the song that Taylor allegedly wrote about Joe, 'Mr. Perfectly Fine' to her Instagram stories, showing that she has no problem with her husband's ex writing songs about him.

This isn't the first time this has happened either, as Sophie also mentioned Taylor's song 'All Too Well' on her Instagram stories and spoke about how good it was. The married couple even attended Taylor's performance of the song on Saturday Night Live, showing that there is clearly no 'Bad Blood' between any of them.

What Songs Did Taylor Swift Write About Joe Jonas?

Due to how upset she was about the breakup, Taylor did the most Taylor Swift thing possible and wrote a few songs about it. There are many stories behind Taylor's songs and the ones that are supposed to be about Joe are some of her best hits to date.

One of these is her song 'Forever & Always' off her album Fearless. The title is allegedly words that Joe said to Taylor during their relationship, but the song then goes on to call him out on failing to stick to his promise and for dumping her over the phone.

Given the relationship ended 15 years ago, people might assume that Taylor would be over it, but it's not always easy to get over a first love. Many believe that her song 'Invisible String' from her album 'Folklore' is about Joe, as it talks about the past and that she now sends "their babies presents."

These only go to show that despite her maybe wanting to hide some of the meanings behind her songs, they appear to be much more revealing than she originally intended.
