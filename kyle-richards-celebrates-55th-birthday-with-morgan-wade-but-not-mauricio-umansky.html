Kyle Richards celebrates 55th birthday in Mexico with Morgan Wade and pals but not Mauricio U

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

Kyle Richards had some fun in the sun as she celebrated her 55th birthday with Morgan Wade — and without husband Mauricio Umansky.

The “Real Housewives of Beverly Hills” star was photographed enjoying a beach day in Punta Mita, Mexico, on Tuesday, two days ahead of her birthday, with the musician and some pals.

She rocked a black-and-white bikini and a beige cowgirl hat as she lounged in the sun, while Wade, 29, wore black board shorts and a black bikini top under a white tank top.

The birthday trip without Umansky, 53, in sight preceded Wednesday’s episode of “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills,” during which Richards expressed her frustrations about random women DMing her husband despite knowing he’s married.

“Mo gets a lot of DMs from women. They don’t care that he’s married, and they’re always the aggressor,” she vented.

“It just makes you realize there’s a lot of f—king a—holes out there.”

.

It’s unclear exactly when the episode was filmed, but it was likely before Page Six confirmed in July 2023 that the couple had separated after 27 years of marriage.

And although Umansky also confirmed in September 2023 that he and Richards were indeed spending time apart, he later told Page Six that their marriage wasn’t completely over.

“We’re struggling. We’ve been very open about it,” he noted. “We’re separated [but] we’re not throwing in the towel yet.”

Richards later established she still cared about the Agency CEO in October 2023 she admitted it was “hard to see” pictures of him holding hands with his “Dancing with the Stars” Season 32 partner, Emma Slater.

However, fans are convinced she has moved on with Wade despite her denying they’re nothing more than friends.

The pair have seemingly been attached at the hip since the summer and even debuted matching criss-cross heart tattoos and rings within the last year.

During an Amazon Live session this week, Richards admitted she felt guilty for dragging the country crooner into the Bravo spotlight because she doesn’t like being “talked about.”

“She would tell me, ‘It’s not your fault, it’s not your fault.’ Of course, I feel bad because it gave her anxiety, you know?” she further explained.

“Seeing these headlines, and like I said, just thrust into the spotlight brought her anxiety.”
