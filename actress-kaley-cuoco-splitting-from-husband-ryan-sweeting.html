Actress Kaley Cuoco Splitting From Husband Ryan Sweeting

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Actress Kaley Cuoco and Ryan Sweeting announced Friday that they are splitting up after 21 months of marriage.

"Kaley Cuoco and Ryan Sweeting have mutually decided to end their marriage," Cuoco's rep told People. "They ask for privacy at this time. No further statement will be issued regarding this matter."

Cuoco didn't stay away from the public eye for long. Right after the big announcement, she went on a girls night out, according to Hollywood Life. Then, she posted selfies of herself and her pals having fun on Instagram.

She seems to have had a change of heart and has taken down post-breakup social media posts.

The Big Bang Theory star, 29, has unfollowed her ex on social media and has taken down some images of Sweeting on Instagram. She removed an Instagram post from April in which she denied rumors that her marriage was in trouble.

Cuoco has opted to keep photos of the couple during happier times.

Tennis pro Sweeting, 28, went further and has removed all photos of his soon to be ex-wife from social media.

The couple had a whirlwind romance and got engaged in Sept. 2013 after only three months of dating. They married soon after on New Year's Eve.

Further details about the breakup and reasons for the split have not been publicly announced.

Source: People, Hollywood Life
