Molly-Mae Hague Shows Off Her Thousands Of Dollars Designer Handbag

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Molly-Mae is the creative director of the fast-fashion brand PrettyLittleThing - The fashion model and celebrity, who recently released her most recent edit with the brand, posed for a new Instagram photo on Thursday.

Molly-Mae was seen sitting in her Range Rover car, keeping it casual in leggings and an oversized blazer.

With her long blonde hair pulled back into a chic bun, the model posed for the camera wearing oversized sunglasses and a wide smile.

Former Love Island star and model, who met her boyfriend Tommy Fury on the ITV2 dating show, is seen wearing her designer trainers as well as her $5,200 Chanel bag as she rests it on her lap.

Molly-Mae finished off her look with a gray t-shirt and a light, neutral palette of make-up.
