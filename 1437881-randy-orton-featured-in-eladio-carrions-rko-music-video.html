Randy Orton Featured In Eladio Carrins RKO Music Video

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 8, 2024

Randy Orton‘s RKO has become increasingly popular outside the world of professional wrestling.

The Viper’s RKO outta nowhere became a meme in recent years that found major popularity online featuring the WWE Superstar hitting random RKOs on people in real life.

But now Orton’s RKO is featured in Eladio Carrión’s new music video titled after The Legend Killer’s finishing maneuver.

In the video, Orton hits an RKO on a member of the media who was bothering Eladio while standing on a baseball mound.

Afterward, there is a hilarious exchange between Orton and Carrión where the WWE Superstar fanboys over the musical artist and asked to get his autograph on a baseball cap.

You can check out the music video in the embedded video below.

Later this month, Randy Orton will compete in a fatal four-way for the WWE Universal Championship at the Royal Rumble.
