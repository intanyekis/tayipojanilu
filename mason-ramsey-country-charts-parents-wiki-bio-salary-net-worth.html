Mason Ramsey of 11 years old Is Newly But Still Famous on Country Charts. Lets Know about his

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Mason Ramsey, now 12 years old is an American country music singer. He turned out to be a country music star from a social media sensation.

He is popularly recognized as The Walmart Yodeling Boy. Ramsey got a chance in Big Loud Records in 2018 after being popular on the Internet through a viral video of him singing Hank Williams’ “Lovesick Blues” in a Walmart.

Manson’s Birth Facts, Family

Mason Ramsey was born in the United States of America on 16th November 2006. His zodiac sign is Scorpio which defines him as one with a magnetic personality. The popular singer Ramsay started living with his grandparents Ernest and Francis Ramsey at just three weeks old cause of his parent’s alienation.

The further records of his parents are under review at the current time. Furthermore, he likes to play basketball and loves basketball shoes. Once in an interview, he said,

“I used to be the Number One scorer in 5th grade. I used to, yeah. I could always shoot deep. But now, I have no one to play with.”

Manson’s Career, Net worth, Salary

Ramsay started his singing career at the age of four singing for Josh Turner and Gene Watson at Carson Centre. He sang on a Christmas TV show with Kenny Rogers at the age of seven.

However, he became internationally recognized only after singing and yodeling in Harrisburg-based Walmart which went viral on YouTube, Facebook, and Twitter. He was entitled to ‘The Walmart Yodeling Boy’ from this act. Currently, he has signed a record deal with Big Loud and Atlantic Records.

Ramsey talks about his newfound fame:

“It has been kind a hard to get around cause it’s a bunch of people who want to take pictures.”

He got the chance to release his debut single, Famous in 2018. He also performed with DJ Ethan Snoreck at Coachella in the same year 2018.
