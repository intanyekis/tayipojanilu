Who is Denée Benton?

Denée Benton is an American actress and singer.

She earned a Tony Award nomination for her appearance as Natasha Rostova in 2016 Broadway’s Natasha, Pierre, & The Great Comet of 1812.

Besides that, her television appearance includes UnREAL and The Gilded Age.
