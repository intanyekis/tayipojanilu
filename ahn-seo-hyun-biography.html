Who is Ahn Seo-hyun?

Ahn Seo-hyun is a South Korean child actor who does not utilize social media platforms. Furthermore, she is well-known for her roles in Okja (2017), The Housemaid (2010), and The Yellow Sea (2010).

She is handled under the Jikim Entertainment agent.

She was recently seen in Oh! My Ghost in 2022, and she is also working on her future film, The Trouble with Zombies, which is now in pre-production.

Ahn Seo-hyun: Professional Life, Career

In terms of her professional career, Ahn Seo-hyun debuted as a child actor in 2008. Her first film appearance was as a young May Smith in the film Maybe (Rabbit and Lizard).

Love Marriage and Terroir, her first television series, aired the same year.

Then, in 2009, she played Young Yoon Ha-na in Can Anyone Love? and Young Yoon Ha-na in Soul.

Then, in 2010, she was given the opportunity to demonstrate her ability in the television series Three Sisters and the drama special The Freat Gye Choon-bin. In the same year, she appeared in films such as The Housemaid, Man of Vendetta, and The Yellow Sea.
