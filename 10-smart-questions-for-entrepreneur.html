10 Smart Questions for Entrepreneur to Ask Before Starting a Business

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, May 25, 2024

If you are interested in starting your own business rather than working for someone else then questions with entrepreneurs are the best way to find out what it takes to be a successful entrepreneur and learn more about their experience. These kinds of discussions can also help you make a well-informed choice about whether running your business suits into your vision for your life and career.  

If you are thinking starting your own business and you are not sure which direction to take in your existing business, informational interviews give you an opportunity to ask a few questions that can benefit you to explore your options.  

Many successful entrepreneurs take their life and business experiences and monetize them in the form of books, online courses, and more.  

If you get the chance to pick their intellect face-to-face, you should never pass it up. The following are the top 10 questions that you should ask an entrepreneur whenever you get a chance. 

What are some of the mistakes you wished you could’ve avoided?

When you first start a business, you are going to make blunders. It is inescapable. The fewer mistakes that you make, the better it will be.  

If you are really smart, you will pay notice all of the mistakes that businesses before you have made, and bypass them like the disease.  

The best way to discover about some of these easily avoidable mistakes is to ask an entrepreneur.  

The pain of failure is stubborn to forget, and they will always have a few stories of how they got themselves into a solution and what they managed to get themselves back out.  

How to manage misfortune and doubt?

Your friends will doubt you; your family will encourage you to make the safe move, and strangers you have never even met will sit around planning on your downfall.  

The business world is not a very favorable one, and any salty businessman who’s lived to tell the tale will have a few stories about how to manage misfortune and defeat both self-doubt and doubt from others.  

Often, they will give you the motivation that you want to keep your head up and keep moving forward.  

Paul, founder of PJG Financial Limited, a firm dedicated to helping people in the UK find the cheapest loans available to them gave us the following advice: “Keep things simple, what’s the end goal and is what you’re doing now really going to get you closer? Simplify and focus on what’s important.”  

How can be a good leader?

Now that we have learned that you require a team, you require to learn some good leadership skills to keep them motivated and working just as hard as you are. Leadership skills are among the best things that you can receive from a fellow entrepreneur, especially if they are older and have more knowledge than you.  

What is the best approach to marketing?

This question will always produce exciting consequences. Depending on their niche and business they may reveal that they get more business from referrals or that they may get more from social media marketing. Try to learn some knowledge about the way that they developed their own marketing strategy, and use it when forming your own.  

How to know when you have the right idea?

One of the unfortunate things about having a business-oriented mindset is that we are creative people. People often call us “the jack of all trades.” Every day, we come up with new business views and how we can go about executing them as soon as possible.  

With all of these fresh ideas, it can be difficult to figure out which one to zero-in and focus on.  

Every entrepreneur has gone through this time at least once. Asking them how they determined where to focus their focus could enlighten you as to what you should concentrate on yourself.  

What to learn from failures? 

Failures are different from mistakes. Every entrepreneur will make some blunders during their lives, but these are normally easy to recover from and work as important lessons for what not to do in the future. A businessman who can heal from a total failure and strengthen themselves is somebody who you definitely want to have a conversation with.  

The knowledge you will learn out of this particular conversation could surely be worth more than any of the other questions listed above. The next time that you hit by difficult situations, you will be equipped and prepared to fight yourself out of the failure and keep on working towards greatness.  

Who is my ideal customer?

Identifying your customers means going deeper than their primary information and understanding their motivations, pain points and personalities. These audience personas can help you develop products to fit their requirements and market to them in the best ways possible.  

How will I market my business?

Knowing your target audience will also help you know how to market your business. Before you start a business, it is necessary to understand where and how you are going to market that company. The best brands in the world have selected how to deliver their value proposition efficiently. Take time before you begin to develop a marketing strategy that highlights your unique selling proposition to the right people. Inefficient marketing can be the destruction of new businesses.  

When will i turn a profit?

Turn a profit may depend on the funding option. Some investors will not expect you to start paying them for a few years, which provides you the adaptability to grow your business. However, this expectation also comes with necessary interest payments. Conversely, if you are self-funded, you will want to immediately turn a profit so that you can pay your bills and avoid liability. Knowing your profit goals can help you budget efficiently and maximize your profit.  

Will my business have a soft or hard launch?

This question will decide how much adaptability you have for setbacks. For example, if you are scheduling a hard launch for an event, such as an ice cream store trying to catch increased summer business, then you can’t manage to fall behind on your opening schedule. However, if you are working while starting your business or if you are adjustable with the opening date, then you can take more time to improve your business plan before accepting customers.  

Entrepreneurs are hard-working people. If you get an opportunity to come across some of the most successful ones, you will be fortunate if they give you more than a couple minutes of their free time. You probably will not have the chance to ask them all 10 of these questions in an interview format, but you should always have a some of the important ones holding on the tip of your tongue.  

After talking with them and getting their answer, you should make it a point to pen it down in a sketchbook, notepad, or record it on your phone. This will help you to remember the discussion and will give you a helpful source to look back on in the future.
