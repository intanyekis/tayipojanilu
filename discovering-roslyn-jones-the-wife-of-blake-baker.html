Discovering Roslyn Jones, the Wife of Blake Baker

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

Roslyn Jones, the wife of American football coach Blake Baker, shares the same passion for football. Despite her husband’s coaching profession, she has a remarkable soccer background as a former LSU soccer player. Baker, with a birthdate of July 10, 1982, currently holds the role of defensive coordinator on the Mizzou coaching staff. Roslyn Jones, his wife, made her mark as an All-SEC soccer player at LSU, setting a record for the most games played (82) in the history of the LSU soccer team. Before joining the Missouri Tigers, Blake Baker served as the defensive coordinator for the Miami Hurricanes and the Louisiana Tech Tigers.

He completed his education at Tulane University, where he was a four-year letterman, graduating in 2008. Furthermore, Baker’s journey in football began as a three-year starter at linebacker and later as a wide receiver at Houston’s Clear Springs High School. His coaching career commenced as a graduate assistant for the Texas Longhorns. Notably, he served as the safeties coach at Arkansas State in 2013, contributing to the team’s Sun Belt co-championship and GoDaddy Bowl victories. During his tenure at Louisiana Tech, the Bulldogs clinched the Conference USA Western Division championship twice, tallying 42 wins, including nine wins in three consecutive seasons from 2014 to 2016.

Exploring Roslyn Jones’ Marriage to Blake Baker

Roslyn, a former LSU soccer player, was born on May 7, 1987, to Frank and Sherri Jones. During her time at Fontainebleau High School, she excelled, earning a 3.82 GPA and achieving accolades such as Louisiana High School Player of the Year. Her prowess extended to the Baton Rouge Soccer Club, where she led the team to the 2004 Louisiana Club State Championship and the quarterfinals of Region III. At LSU, she initially played on the defensive line before transitioning to the forward position in her second year. Finishing the season as the team’s second-highest scorer, she notched nine goals and 21 points, leaving her mark in the record books by ranking in the top ten at LSU in seven separate offensive statistics.

Roslyn and her husband, Blake Baker, form a formidable athletic couple, with her prominent soccer career complementing his coaching success.

The Married Life of Blake Baker and Roslyn Jones

Blake Baker thrives as a football coach, while Roslyn Jones made her mark as a standout college soccer player. Despite their public personas in sports and coaching, Blake takes pride in showcasing his wife and children on Instagram. The couple, blessed with a girl named Anderson and twin boys Byron and Brady, is often featured in Blake’s social media posts where he expresses his love and admiration for his family.

Roslyn, on the other hand, prefers to maintain her privacy and does not engage in social media. However, fans appreciate the family photos shared by Blake, offering a glimpse into their lives. It seems that Blake enjoys providing his fans with insight into his personal life, in addition to his professional achievements, fostering a stronger connection with his audience.
