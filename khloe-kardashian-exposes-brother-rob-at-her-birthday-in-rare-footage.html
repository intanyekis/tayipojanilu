Khloe Kardashian Exposes Brother Rob At Her Birthday In Rare Footage

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 29, 2024

Highlights

  • Rob Kardashian attended Khloe's 40th birthday party, signaling a possible return to the spotlight.
  • Rob was featured in a recent episode of "The Kardashians" discussing sperm donation for Khloe's friend Malika.
  • Rob has been largely out of the public eye since his 2016 breakup with Blac Chyna, but recent appearances may indicate a comeback.

It looks like everyone turned out to celebrate Khloe Kardashian’s 40th birthday, including her younger brother, Rob Kardashian, who infamously avoids the spotlight nowadays.

On Thursday, June 27, Khloé celebrated her birthday by sharing a collection of photos and videos on Instagram. The compilation featured nostalgic images from various stages of her life, along with a heartwarming scene of Khloé blowing out birthday candles to mark her 40th year.

How Rob Helped Khloe Celebrate Her 40th Birthday

She was surrounded by her brother Rob, 37, her grandmother MJ Campbell, and her close friend Malika Haqq. The family affair included hilarious decorations, as Khloe used her 2009 mug shot to adorn the party utensils and favors, a throwback to a wilder time in Khloe’s life.

Rob expressed his affection in the comments, writing, “Happy Birthday scooby boobie.” Khloé responded with equal warmth, saying, “I love you my favorite human.”

It seems like Rob didn’t mind having his cover blown at Khloe’s party. There’s often speculation that Rob is in the background at Kardashian-Jenner events, but he’s rarely shown camera-facing, given that he’s known for preferring to stay out of the spotlight in recent years.

Rob Appeared On ‘The Kardashians’ Recently

Close

This wasn’t Rob’s only sighting recently, perhaps signaling he’s planning to be more active in the public eye again. Earlier this month, Rob was featured in a The Kardashians episode when Khloe called to ask if he’d donate sperm to her friend Malika – seriously.

Rob and Malika have history, with their flirtatiousness documented on past seasons of Keeping Up with the Kardashians. Malika is currently looking to have a second baby using a donor, since her relationship with her first child’s father didn’t work out. And as Khloe and Malika both acknowledge, her biological clock is ticking, so time is of the essence.

Khloe later revealed to the camera that Malika and Rob hooked up before, so she didn’t think her request was too big or weird to ask her little brother, though he didn’t see it that way.

Rob quickly shut Khloe down, expressing as much disgust as viewers had while watching the uncomfortable scene. But when his sister persists, Rob reveals he couldn’t even if he wanted to, as he can no longer perform like he once could… whatever that means.

The Arthur George founder has largely been out of the spotlight since his relationship with Blac Chyna broke down in 2016.

They share 8-year-old daughter Dream together. Rob faced legal consequences after their nasty split went online, with the reality star sharing nudes as well as Chyna’s phone number amid substance abuse allegations and claims she cheated.

While Dream has become a fixture on her family’s reality show and social media channels, Rob reeled back following the public spat with Chyna. But his recent appearances could be a sign he’s launching a comeback or that Kris Jenner trusts him enough to let him into the limelight.
