Madison De La Garza Wiki Biography

Madison Lee De La Garza was born on 28 December 2001, in Dallas, Texas USA, of Mexican descent, and is an actress, best known for being a part of the show “Desperate Housewives” in which she plays Juanita Solis. She’s been active in the industry since 2008, and all of her efforts have helped put her net worth to where it is today.

How rich is Madison De La Garza? As of early-2018, sources estimate a net worth that is at $700,000, mostly earned through a successful career in acting. As she continues her endeavors, it is expected that her wealth will also continue to increase.

Madison is the half-sister of singer and songwriter Demi Lovato. Her first appearance on television was in an episode of “Jonas Brothers: Living the Dream” in 2008, then during the same year, she was cast in a recurring role in the television show “Desperate Housewives”, appearing in it from the fifth to the eighth seasons; the series is set in a fictional town and follows the lives of a group of women and is seen through their late friend who committed suicide in the pilot episode. More opportunities started to come her way which increased her net worth – while a part of “Desperate Housewives”, Madison also appeared in an episode of “Sonny with a Chance”, playing a younger version of Demi Lovato’s character. Afterwards, she became a part of the television film “Princess Protection Program”, before appearing in the documentary “Demi Lovato: Stay Strong” which follows her half-sister’s story after leaving rehab, seeking treatment for depression and self-harm. Her next major projects would come in 2014, when she became a part of an episode of “Good Luck Charlie”, and also had a role in the television series “Bad Teacher” which is loosely based on the 2011 film of the same name, however, the show was cancelled after three episodes.

In 2016, De La Garza made her film debut in “Cage No More”, which also stars Kevin Sorbo and Loretta Devine, followed by an appearance in the documentary “Simply Complicated”, before trying her hand at voice-acting for the film “Gnome Alone”, based on the book by Jared Micah Herman and Kyle Newman.
