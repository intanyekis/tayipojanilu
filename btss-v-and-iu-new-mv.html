BTS's V and IU to Bring Magic to the Screen in New Music Video!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 2, 2024

Big Hit Music, V’s agency, added on the 5th, “It is right for him to appear in IU’s new song music video.” Along with that, Edam Entertainment, IU’s agency, announced, “IU has finished filming the music video with BTS’s V, and is preparing a new album with a target of the first half of the year.”

Take a look at below tweet:

BIGHIT MUSIC and EDAM Entertainment confirms that BTS’s V will star in IU’s comeback music video, set to release in the 1st half of 2024.https://t.co/GhoY0kSFlv pic.twitter.com/HzUMxiPAJP

— Kpop Charts (@kchartsmaster) December 5, 2023

The agency continued by saying, “Currently, IU is planning to spend the end of this year with a busy schedule, with the final filming of the drama ‘I was fooled’ and the preparation of a new album.” Anticipate it with pleasure. With the hope of releasing it in the middle of next year, IU is currently hard at work on new music. But because V’s enlistment is scheduled for the 11th, the music video had to be shot before V’s schedule could be accommodated.

V made an appearance on ‘IU’s Palette’ in September of last year, bragging about his unique friendship with IU and drawing attention to the fact that the two spoke casually.

Since IU has been silent since the release of ‘Piece of Pieces’ in December 2021, fans are eagerly awaiting the announcement of her new album. In addition, on the 11th and 12th, all members of BTS (of which V is a part), RM, V, Jimin, and Jungkook will enlist in the military, with the exception of Suga, Jin, and J-Hope, who are already serving.
