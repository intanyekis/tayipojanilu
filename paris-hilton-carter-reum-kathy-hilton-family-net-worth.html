How Much Is Everyone Worth In 2021?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Reality television star and DJ Paris Hilton rose to fame in the early 2000s and ever since then she has managed to have more and more success. Last year, Paris was in the spotlight due to her documentary This Is Paris, and this year her reality television show Paris in Love premiered. Besides reality television, Paris has established herself as a respected DJ during the 2010s.

Related: Paris Hilton Celebrates Her Marriage With A Single Wedding Photograph.... Without Her Husband

Today, we're taking a look at just how rich Paris Hilton's family is. From her brand new husband Carter Reum to her siblings Nicky and Barron to her reality TV star mother Kathy Hilton — keep on scrolling to see just how much each of the reality television star's family members are worth in 2021!

7 Paris Hilton Has A Net Worth Of $300 Million

Let's kick the list off with the fact that Paris Hilton is currently estimated to have a net worth of $300 million. The socialite rose to fame in the early 2000s with the reality television show The Simple Life which she starred in alongside Nicole Richie. Since then, Paris has managed to establish herself as a media personality, successful businesswoman, model, singer, DJ, and actress. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Paris Hilton is currently estimated to have an impressive net worth of $300 million.

6 Her Husband Carter Reum Is Estimated To Have A Net Worth Between $35 And $40 Million

Next on the list is Paris Hilton's husband — entrepreneur and venture capitalist Carter Reum. The two tied the know this fall in a star-studded ceremony. The two have been dating since May 2019 and in February 2021 the couple got engaged.

Related: Paris Hilton Calls Her Simple Life ‘Iconic’ After Finding A Halloween Costume Of Herself

The reality television show Paris In Love follows the two lovebirds as they prepare for their big day. Currently, Carter Reum is estimated to have a net worth between $35 and $40 million.

5 Paris' Sister Nicky Hilton Has A Net Worth Of $50 Million

Let's move on to Paris' younger sister Nicky Hilton who is currently estimated to have a net worth of $50 million. Today, Nicky is known as a businesswoman, socialite, model, and fashion designer. However, while Paris Hilton seems to thrive in the spotlight, Nicky Hilton tends to prefer staying out of the limelight. She has been married to financier James Rothschild since 2015 and together they have two daughters — Lily Grace Victoria Rothschild born in 2016 and Theodora "Teddy" Marilyn Rothschild born in 2017.

4 Her Parents Kathy And Richard Hilton Have A Combined Net Worth Of $350 Million

Of course, this list would not be complete without Paris Hilton's parents — Kathy and Richard Hilton. As those who keep up with the Hiltons know, Richard Hilton is a successful businessman and chairman and co-founder of Hilton & Hyland, while Kathy Hilton is currently best known for starring on the popular reality television show The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. Together, the two have an estimated net worth of $350 million.

3 Paris' Brother Barron Hilton II Has A Net Worth Of $5 Million

Next on the list is Paris Hilton's younger brother Barron Hilton II who certainly prefers staying out of the limelight.

Paris' younger brother spends a majority of his time focusing on his family — he is married to German countess Tessa Gräfin von Walderdorff and together the two have a daughter. Currently, Barron Hilton II is estimated to have a net worth of $5 million. Besides Barron, Paris Hilton has another brother — Conrad Hughes Hilton, however, his net worth is currently not disclosed.

2 Her Aunt Kyle Richards Has A Net Worth Of $100 Million

Let's move on to Paris Hilton's aunt Kyle Richards. Kyle — who is Kathy Hilton's sister — is currently best known for starring in every season of the reality television show The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. However, the star has been in the spotlight for most of her life as she kicked her career off as a child actress. Some of the projects that Kyle Richards starred in are Little House on the Prairie, The Car, Tobe Hooper's Eaten Alive, The Watcher In The Woods, and Halloween. Currently, Kyle Richards is estimated to have a net worth of $100 million.

1 And Her Other Aunt Kim Richards Has A Net Worth Of $400 Thousand

And finally, wrapping the list up is Paris Hilton's other aunt and Kathy Hilton's sister — Kim Richards. Just like Kyle, Kim also grew up as a child actress and she appeared in projects like Nanny and the Professor, Escape to Witch Mountain, and Return from Witch Mountain. Besides this, Kim Richards has also appeared in multiple seasons of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. Currently, Kim is estimated to have a net worth of $400,000 which means that she is certainly not as rich as the rest of the family.
