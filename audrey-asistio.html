How did Audrey Asistio start her Professional Career?

Asistio currently works for NBC Bay Area. In October 2021, she joined the channel as a weekend anchor. She also co-anchors the 5 p.m., 6 p.m., and 11 p.m. newscasts with Terry McSweeney. Before joining NBC Bay Area, Asistio worked for NBC10 Boston for more than five years.

She began working as a full-time news anchor for NBC10 Boston in August 2016. Audrey Asistio covered a number of major news events there, including the 2018 Winter Olympics in South Korea, the 2020 Summer Olympics in Japan, and George Flyod’s assassination in 2020. She was also the only reporter to provide a live stream of the 2017 Halloween Day terrorist attack.

Asistio has previously worked with a variety of television networks. She worked as a media journalist and fill-in anchor for KSEE Television in Fresno, California, from April 2011 to January 2013. From January 2013 to June 2016, she worked as a full-time news anchor for KOVR-TV in Sacramento.
